# KPI Definitions — Demand Planning Platform

## Homepage KPIs

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Top 3 Practices | Practices contributing most to overall demand forecast | Practice names with % of demand |
| Top 5 Skill Micro Clusters | Skill Micro Clusters with highest forecast FTE aggregated over next 12 months | List of Skill Micro Cluster Names |

## Forecast Overview KPIs

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Total Forecast Demand (FTEs) | Total forecasted FTE demand across the next 12 months | # of FTEs |
| Demand Growth Rate (MoM, QoQ, WoW) | % change in overall demand forecast | % |
| Average Cancellation % | % of cancellation demand in a month | % |

## Demand Type Breakdown KPIs

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| % New Demand vs Backfill | How much demand is from new demand vs backfill based on requirement type | % |
| Contract Type Mix (T&M / Fixed Price / Transaction) | Share of forecasted demand by different billability types | % |

## Location Mix KPIs

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Onsite / Offshore Ratio % | Forecasted mix of FTE demand by onsite vs offshore | % |
| Top 5 Countries for Delivery | Countries with highest aggregated forecast over next 12 months | List of Countries with % of FTE demand |

## Skill Mix KPIs

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Top 3 Skill Micro Clusters within Top Practice Area | Skill Micro Clusters within top practice area with highest forecast FTE | List with % demand |
| Top 3 Skill Micro Clusters in SL | Skill Micro Clusters with highest forecast FTE in Service Line | List with % demand |
| % Stable & Volatile Demand Volume | Share of forecasted demand in stable vs volatile skill micro clusters | % |

## Business Unit KPIs (Markets Persona Only)

| KPI | Description | Units |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Top 3 BUs by Demand | BUs with highest total forecast FTE demand over next 12 months | BU Names with % |
| Top 3 BUs by Growth Rate | Fastest growing BUs | BU Names with growth % |
